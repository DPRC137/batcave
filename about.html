About the Batcave

The Batcave is more than just a secret lair—it's a sanctuary of innovation. Hidden beneath Wayne Manor, this hideaway combines cutting-edge technology with timeless design.

From high-tech workstations to the iconic Batmobile bay, every corner serves a purpose. Hidden passageways and state-of-the-art security keep intruders at bay while allowing quick access to Gotham's streets.

At the heart of the cave runs WayneTech OS 7.4, enabling advanced surveillance and forensic tools directly from the terminal.
